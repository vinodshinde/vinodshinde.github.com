Privacy Policy

  • "To-Do with Alerts" app does not upload or share user's personal data or information with any 3rd parties or websites or servers.
    All the data is saved locally on user's device..
  • "To-Do with Alerts" app does not track physical location of the user.
  • SkyDrive access: "To-Do with Alerts" app does not automatically upload or download any data or personal information from user's SkyDrive. Backup to SkyDrive is uploaded only when user explicitly starts the backup process. Backup's are uploaded to "To-Do with Alerts" folder in SkyDrive.
  • We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy.
    You can always access the current version of the privacy policy at http://vinodshinde.github.io/todowithalerts/privacy.html
